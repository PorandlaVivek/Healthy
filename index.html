Healthy Diet

General Healthy Eating

Foods to Eat

  • Fruits: Apples, bananas, berries, oranges, mangoes
  • Vegetables: Leafy greens, carrots, broccoli, bell peppers
  • Whole Grains: Brown rice, quinoa, oats, whole wheat bread
  • Lean Proteins: Chicken breast, fish, tofu, beans
  • Healthy Fats: Avocados, nuts, seeds, olive oil

Foods to Avoid

  • Processed Foods: Chips, cookies, frozen meals
  • Sugary Drinks: Sodas, energy drinks

Dietary Preferences

Vegan Diet

  • To Eat: Tofu, legumes, quinoa, vegetables, fruits
  • To Avoid: Animal products, dairy, eggs, honey

Keto Diet

  • To Eat: Meat, fish, eggs, low-carb vegetables, cheese
  • To Avoid: Sugar, grains, high-carb fruits, legumes

Specific Health Conditions

Diabetes

  • To Eat: Whole grains, lean protein, non-starchy vegetables
  • To Avoid: Sugary foods, refined carbs, sugary drinks

Heart Health

  • To Eat: Oats, berries, fatty fish, nuts
  • To Avoid: Trans fats, high-sodium foods, red meat
